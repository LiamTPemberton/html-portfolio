The Best Movies According to Liam

My top 3 movies of all timw

Dead Mans Shoes

Gripping and dark physcological revenge thriller. There's a fantastic twist at the end and the acting is superb.

Forrest Gump

A wonderful story about finding your way in the world. We follow Forrest from childhood as he makes his way into some of the biggest moments in U.S History.

This is Spinal Tap

The funniest film I have ever seen. I quote it all the time. Following the mishaps of a once great band on a U.S tour. The songs are actually fantastic too.
